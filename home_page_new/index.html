About Me

I am studying for a Ph.D in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) and the Institute of Future City (IOFC). at The Chinese University of Hong Kong (CUHK).



I received my BSc degrees in Computer Engineering from The Chinese University of Hong Kong and Electronic Information Science and Technology from Sun Yan-Sen University (SYSU) in July 2014. Further I spent one year working in the Intel Embedded Systems Research Center at the Department of Computer Science and Engineering of the Chinese University of Hong Kong.
